Why LM²7?

Our team name, , has a deeper meaning than just initials.
The “L” represents Leda, “M²” represents Malik and Moayad, “7” representing Hala and “7” makes a “ح” sound that is taken from the Arabic character. Furthermore, the combination of these components becomes “LM²7”, which means “Glance” in Arabic. This displays our team’s ability to catch attention and make an impact at first glance, demonstrating our dedication to innovation and quality.

04

What is F1 in Schools?

F1 in Schools is a global educational program where students aged 9-19 design and race miniature F1 cars. Teams use CAD software to create their cars, which are then manufactured and raced on a special track. The competition helps students develop skills in engineering, teamwork, and project management, preparing them for careers in STEM fields.
